About Tussen De Vaarten

Tussen De Vaarten is located in the East part of Almere City (Stad) in the Flevoland District with a population of almost 16,000. The area is divided into 3 main parts; Noord, Zuid and Midden. The name “Tussen De Vaarten” means “Between the Canals” in Dutch.

Distance from Almere Centrum: Around 3.7km
