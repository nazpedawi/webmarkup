Activities for Kids

Yvette DansStudio

Yvette dance studio is located in Tussen De Vaarten Noord. They give dance lessons to toddlers, kids and even adults, they teach hip-hop, modern dance, ballet and much more! They also participate in dance competitions all around the Netherlands. It is possible to take a trail class for free and see if it is something you or your children would like to do. You can contact them and learn all about them through the link provided below.

Aquayara Zwemschool/Swimming School

Swimming is an essential skill that everyone should have, for survival in emergency situations and for having fun in water in the summer while also staying safe! this swimming school gives swimming lessons not only to kids but to adults as well. Your child can achieve the level A, B and C swimming diplomas for survival at this school. It is also possible for your child to take swimming lessons in small groups. They have 3 locations, this one is located in Tussen De Vaarten Midden. For more information and contact details visit their website provided below.

Activities for People with Mental Disabilities

Freeway Zorg

Freeway Zorg offers Housing, Assisted living and daytime activities for people with mental disabilities. They provide 24 hour support and protection for their clients. During the day the clients can do various activities and work such as bicycle maintenance and repairing, woodwork, arts and crafts, pottery and many more. The activities are chosen based on the client’s needs and interests. The client can also do training and move on to a paid job which helps them develop their skills on the labour market. For more information and contact details visit the link provided.

Freeway Zorg Website

Het Kan! Day Activities

Het Kan is one of ‘S Heeren Loo’s many locations, which help people of all ages with intellectual disability and other disabilities live their lives the way they want with support and care from supervisors. At Het Kan, the clients can express their creativity through crafts, paintings, drawing or whatever the client wants to create that can be sold, because Het Kan has a shop where clients can sell their own products and their products are also sold at Christmas Markets. There is a nursing home nearby where most of the clients live. Both buildings are in a relaxed area near the river with a nice path for clients to walk on between their work and home. For more information visit their website.
